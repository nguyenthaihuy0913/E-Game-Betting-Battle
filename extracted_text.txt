PART 2: GAP-FILL EXERCISES (20 câu)
Complete each sentence with the correct form of the word(s) in brackets or the appropriate word/phrase from the box.
Word Bank:amenities • vulnerable • pickpockets • get used to • look on the bright side • better safe than sorry • mind their own business • settle in • take some getting used to • all in all • petty crime • well-lit • security cameras • out of their comfort zone • mean well
1. Living in a bustling city can be exciting, but it will ____________________ (take / get / use) for someone from a rural area.
2. ____________________ (both / security cameras / and / guards) help reduce criminal activities in this urban area.
3. I really appreciate ____________________ (neighbors / look out) for each other during the holiday season.
4. You should always walk in ____________________ (well / light) areas to avoid becoming a target for criminals.
5. ____________________ (either / you / settle in) a densely-populated neighborhood or a quiet suburb, you will find both have advantages and disadvantages.
6. The victim didn't notice ____________________ (pickpocket / take) her wallet because she was distracted.
7. What gets on my nerves is ____________________ (people / cut) in line without any consideration for others.
8. The new residents are slowly ____________________ (settle) into the community and joining local activities.
9. ____________________ (not only / theft) but also violent crime have decreased in this area since the new measures were implemented.
10. Even though some neighbors may seem nosy, they usually ____________________ (mean) well.
11. The ____________________ (amenities) in this ideal neighborhood include a gym, a swimming pool, and a community garden.
12. Tourists are often ____________________ (vulnerable) to purse-snatchers because they are distracted by their surroundings.
13. The success of the fundraising event was due to ____________________ (everyone / donate) generously.
14. When moving to a new city, it's important to ____________________ (look / bright side) and focus on the opportunities.
15. ____________________ (neither / the noise) nor the crowded streets bother her now that she is accustomed to urban life.
16. The residents were concerned about ____________________ (strangers / congregate) outside their building late at night.
17. I don't mind ____________________ (neighbors / mind) their own business, but I appreciate when they offer help when needed.
18. ____________________ (both / high-rise apartment buildings) and well-maintained historical buildings can be found in this area.
19. It's always ____________________ (better / safe / sorry), so keep your valuables in a secure place.
20. ____________________ (all / all), this neighborhood has a lot to offer despite its minor drawbacks.
